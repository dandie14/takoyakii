TAKOYAKI

Info About Product

Takoyaki will be a small business, and our small takoyaki food product business will be offered with a variety of flavors and colors. This product will contain an amazing taste, allowing customers to fill their hunger. Takoyaki will also include a free voucher, providing greater convenience for customers.

The Uniqueness of our Product

The uniqueness of this product is that the takoyaki mixture has been enhanced with food flavor to create different takoyaki balls. Some have crab sticks and cheese inside, while others have bacon and cheese inside. The original brown-colored takoyaki balls can be customized with different toppings depending on customer preferences.
